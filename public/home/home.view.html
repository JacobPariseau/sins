What is Firecup?

Firecup is the ultimate party game with variants ideal for every party -- all the way from quiet movie nights to off-the-hook college parties. Take everything you loved about the rules of King's Cup and Sociables, add a thousand cards, let people play from their own phones, and you've got a drinking game for adults that no other game can compare to.

Watching Movies

If you're having a movie night, we recommend Firetraps Lite with the Movie Traps expansion. You and your friends will have tons of cards with actions that both your friends and the movie characters can trigger. If your friend sets off a card, make her drink. If the movie sets off a card, everyone drinks!

Playing Some Games

Play on shared or individual devices!

When you create a game, your friends can all join in from their own phones, tablets, or laptops, if they prefer.
With the exception of the Firetraps variants (which by necessity require each player to have their own device) players can also share a device and pass it around to whomever's turn it is.
If you're playing on your own device and your phone is about to die, another player can add you to their device in the game settings.
