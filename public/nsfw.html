How does it work?

Once you buy the expansion, it will be linked to your Facebook account. Any game you start while signed into Facebook will have the NSFW theme as a selectable option in the game settings. Some variants, like Traps, only have Trap and Action cards. If you have selected this variant, your deck will be populated with the NSFW Trap and Action cards as well as the cards from the Classic deck.

The cards are all cross-compatible with other variants, so while the NSFW Trap cards are designed to be used on other players, they will still work on the Movie Night variant where every player takes a drink when a movie character triggers a trap.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game better to play with friends or strangers?

That depends on the quality of friends or strangers you're playing with. As always, exercise caution and never put yourself in a position of danger.

What if I don't want to do what a card says?

It's never okay to feel forced into doing something, so you can always opt out if you want. A common house rule is that you can get out of a card by finishing your drink, but that's something to decide amongst your party.
